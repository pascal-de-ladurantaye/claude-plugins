# General Prompt Examples

## Character Consistency

### User Request
"I need thumbnails for my YouTube series featuring the same person"

### Crafted Prompt
```
Create a YouTube thumbnail featuring the person from the reference image. Keep the person's facial features exactly the same as the reference. Scene: they're reacting with exaggerated surprise to a giant golden play button floating beside them. Dynamic lighting with rim light from the play button. Bold text overlay reading "1 MILLION!" in 3D metallic gold letters with red outline. High-energy, clickbait-style composition with vibrant saturated colors. The person should be wearing a casual blue hoodie, mouth open in amazement, hands raised.
```

### Metadata
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Resolution**: 2K
- **Capability Area**: Character Consistency
- **Edit Refinements**:
  - "Make the facial expression more exaggerated"
  - "Add sparkle effects around the play button"
  - "Increase the glow intensity from the button"

## Advanced Manipulation

### User Request
"Remove the tourists from this photo of the Eiffel Tower"

### Crafted Prompt
```
Remove all people and tourists from this photograph of the Eiffel Tower, reconstructing the ground area with contextually appropriate cobblestones, grass, and shadows that would naturally exist in an empty Champ de Mars. Maintain the exact lighting conditions, time of day, and atmospheric quality of the original image. Preserve all architectural details of the tower and surrounding elements.
```

### Metadata
- **Aspect Ratio**: [Match original]
- **Resolution**: [Match original or higher]
- **Capability Area**: Advanced Manipulation
- **Edit Refinements**:
  - "Add a few pigeons for realism"
  - "Adjust shadow direction for consistency"
  - "Enhance the grass texture in foreground"

## Dimensional Translation (2D to 3D)

### User Request
"Turn this floor plan into a realistic interior render"

### Crafted Prompt
```
Convert this architectural floor plan into a photorealistic 3D interior rendering. Camera position: standing in the living room corner, looking toward the kitchen and dining area. Modern Scandinavian interior design with light oak flooring, white walls, and large windows with natural daylight streaming in. Furnish appropriately: gray linen sectional sofa, walnut dining table with 6 chairs, pendant lighting over the kitchen island. Time of day: late afternoon golden hour. Include realistic materials: matte paint walls, brushed nickel fixtures, marble countertops with subtle veining.
```

### Metadata
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Resolution**: 4K
- **Capability Area**: Dimensional Translation
- **Edit Refinements**:
  - "Add plants and decorative objects for warmth"
  - "Adjust window light to be softer"
  - "Include a area rug under the dining table"

## High-Resolution Texture

### User Request
"Detailed macro shot of coffee beans for packaging"

### Crafted Prompt
```
A 4K hyperdetailed macro photograph of freshly roasted arabica coffee beans in a small pile. Extreme close-up showing individual bean surface texture: the natural oils creating subtle sheen, characteristic crack lines from roasting, slight variations in roast color from medium to dark brown. Visible micro-details: tiny chaff particles, surface irregularities, and the matte-to-glossy gradient across each bean. Studio lighting with soft key light from upper left creating gentle shadows between beans. Shallow depth of field with tack-sharp focus on the center beans, creamy bokeh on edges. Suitable for high-end coffee brand packaging print at large scale.
```

### Metadata
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1
- **Resolution**: 4K
- **Capability Area**: High-Resolution Output
- **Edit Refinements**:
  - "Add subtle steam wisps suggesting freshness"
  - "Increase the oil sheen on foreground beans"
  - "Darken the background to pure black"

## Sequential Narrative

### User Request
"Storyboard for a product launch video"

### Crafted Prompt
```
Create a 4-panel cinematic storyboard for a smartphone product launch video. Consistent style throughout: dramatic black background, single spotlight illumination, hyperrealistic product rendering.

Panel 1 (Wide): The phone floating in darkness, single beam of light hitting it from above, subtle lens flare.
Panel 2 (Close-up): Extreme close-up on the camera module, showing lens glass reflections and metallic trim detail.
Panel 3 (Side profile): Thin profile shot emphasizing the slim design, edge lighting creating a glowing outline.
Panel 4 (Hero shot): Phone at 3/4 angle with screen displaying a vibrant abstract wallpaper, reflection on glossy surface below.

Product: flagship smartphone with titanium frame, matte black glass back, triple camera array. Apple-keynote-style presentation aesthetic.
```

### Metadata
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Resolution**: 4K
- **Capability Area**: Sequential Narrative
- **Edit Refinements**:
  - "Add subtle particle dust in the spotlight beams"
  - "Increase the screen brightness in Panel 4"
  - "Add reflection of the phone in Panel 1"

## Structural Layout Control

### User Request
"Turn this napkin sketch into a real ad"

### Crafted Prompt
```
Convert this rough hand-drawn sketch into a polished advertisement for an organic juice brand. Maintain the exact composition and element placement from the sketch: bottle centered, fruit arrangement on the left, tagline position at top, logo placement at bottom right.

Render the juice bottle as a realistic glass bottle with condensation droplets, filled with vibrant orange-mango juice. The fruit arrangement: whole and sliced oranges, mangoes, with scattered mint leaves, all with studio-quality food photography lighting. Background: clean white with subtle gradient.

Typography: tagline "Pure. Fresh. Natural." in elegant serif font (suggest: Playfair Display), brand logo in modern sans-serif. Overall aesthetic: premium organic food brand, Whole Foods Market quality.
```

### Metadata
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:3
- **Resolution**: 4K
- **Capability Area**: Structural Layout Control
- **Edit Refinements**:
  - "Add water splash effect behind the bottle"
  - "Make the condensation droplets more prominent"
  - "Adjust fruit arrangement to feel more dynamic"
